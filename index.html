Fun Facts About Otters:

  • An otter pup's fur is so dense that it can't dive underwater until it gets its adult fur!
  • Sea otter fur is actually the densest of any mammal at about 1 million hairs per square inch!
  • Sea otters eat around 25 percent of their body weight in food every day! (Occasionally they will dive as deep as 250 feet to find food!)

Did You Know?

  • An otter's Lung capacity is 2.5 times greater than that of a similar-sized land mammal
  • Otters are an essential keystone species
  • A group of resting otters is called a raft
